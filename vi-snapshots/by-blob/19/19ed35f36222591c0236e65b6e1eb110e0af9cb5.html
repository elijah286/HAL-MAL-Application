Frequency Measurement.lvlib:Frequency Measurement.lvclass:Limit Test.vi

Takes the raw data from the <b>FreqSweepTest VI</b> and filter error limits and then calculates if the filter response data ever went out of bounds. If they did not, then the limit test passed and the VI outputs a "True" from the "Test Passed" indicator. It also outputs a waveform graph showing the results of the frequency sweep test and the limits.

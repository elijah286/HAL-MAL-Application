Defines what the actor does before it stops. Use the final error code input to determine whether the actor shut down normally. By default, a value of 43 indicates the actor stopped normally. Any other value indicates the actor stopped due to an error. However, descendants of Actor.lvclass can override Actor.lvclass:Handle Error.vi to change this behavior.

By default, this method does nothing. A descendant class may override it to define behavior, such as shutting down any processes the actor initiated in its override of Actor.lvclass:Actor Core.vi.

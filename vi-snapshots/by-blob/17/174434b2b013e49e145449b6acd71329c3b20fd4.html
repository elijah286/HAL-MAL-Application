Diode Characterization.lvlib:Diode IV.lvclass:Peak Detection Helper.vi

Detects up to 10 peaks in the given waveform that are over the given threshold. Output is in the form of an array of annotations that can be displayed on the graph alongside the input waveform.

threshold

threshold instructs the VI to ignore peaks and valleys that are too small. The VI ignores peaks if the fitted amplitude is less than threshold. The VI ignores valleys if the fitted trough is greater than threshold.
